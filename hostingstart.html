Lord Shree Dhanvantari – The Divine Healer & God of Ayurveda

Lord Shree Dhanvantari, the God of Ayurveda, is the divine healer and an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, revered for bringing the knowledge of Ayurveda to the world. He emerged from the Samudra Manthan (churning of the ocean) holding a pot of Amrit (nectar of immortality), symbolizing health and well-being. Worshiped for his blessings in health, longevity, and healing, he is honored during Dhanteras and revered by Ayurveda practitioners worldwide.

ಶಂಖಂ ಚಕ್ರಮುಪರ್ಯಧಶ್ಚ ಕರಯೋರ್ದಿವ್ಯೌಷಧಂ ದಕ್ಷಿಣೇ।
ವಾಮೇನಾನ್ಯಕರೇಣ ಸಂಭ್ರತ ಸುಧಾಕುಂಭಂ ಜ್ವಲತ್ಕಾಪಿಲಂ॥
ಬಿಭ್ರಾಣಃ ಕರುಣಾಕರಃ ಶುಭಕರಃ ಸರ್ವಾಮಯಧ್ವಂಸಕಃ।
ಸರ್ವಂ ಮೇ ದುರಿತಂ ಛಿನತ್ತು ಭಗವಾನ್ ಧನ್ವಂತರಿಃ ಸಂತತಂ ॥

“Holding the conch, discus, leech, and the pot of divine nectar, Lord Dhanvantari, embodiment of compassion and destroyer of all ailments — may He remove all my afflictions.”

ನಮಾಮಿ ಧನ್ವಂಟರಿಮಾದಿದೆವಂ।
ಸುರಾಸುರೈರ್ವಂದಿತಪಾದಪದ್ಮಂ॥
ಲೋಕೆ ಜರಾರುಗ್ಭಯ ಮೃತ್ಯುನಾಶಂ।
ದಾತಾರಮೀಶಂ ವಿಭಿದೌಷಧೀನಾಂ॥

“I bow to Lord Dhanvantari, the primal God, whose lotus feet are worshipped by gods and demons alike. He dispels disease, fear, and death, and bestows the healing herbs that sustain life.”

About the Temple

A serene sanctuary devoted to health, purity, and spiritual rejuvenation. The temple honors Lord Dhanvantari, patron of Ayurveda, with daily rituals, healing sessions, and community programs.
